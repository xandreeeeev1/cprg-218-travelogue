Enchanted Kingdom

Enchanted Kingdom, located in Santa Rosa, Laguna, Philippines, is a captivating theme park that brings fantasy and excitement to life. With a magical blend of thrilling rides, family-friendly attractions, and spectacular performances, the park transports visitors into a world of adventure and enchantment. It features various themed zones, including the mystical "Boulderville," the whimsical "Dynamo," and the exhilarating "Space Shuttle," offering something for every age group. The park is also known for its vibrant festivals and parades, creating a festive atmosphere throughout the year. Whether it's the adrenaline rush of roller coasters or the charm of interactive shows, Enchanted Kingdom provides an unforgettable experience for both locals and tourists alike.

This photo was taken on March 14, 2023. I visited this place to enjoy my vacation in the Philippines. I am with my friends and we try almost every rides in this wonderful place.

Santa Rosa City is where I lived. When someone ask me where I came from, I'd say in Santa Rosa. And the next question they asked me was "So you're always in Enchanted Kingdom?". But just to be honest since I grew up in this place, It's been like 3-4 times since I visited this Theme Park.

Emerald River

The Emerald River in British Columbia, Canada, is a stunning, crystal-clear river known for its vibrant, bright green color. The river flows through a beautiful natural landscape, surrounded by tall trees, mountains, and lush forests. Its unique emerald hue comes from the minerals in the water, creating a mesmerizing sight. The river is a peaceful and serene place, perfect for outdoor activities like hiking, kayaking, and enjoying the fresh air. Visitors often come to admire its beauty and the surrounding wilderness, making it a special and peaceful destination in the heart of nature.
